Travel Tips for Cambodia

Domestic Travel

  • Book flights at least 2 weeks in advance for best prices
  • VIP buses offer more comfort for long journeys
  • Consider travel time when choosing between flight and bus
  • Check visa requirements for international connections

International Connections

  • Arrive at airports 3 hours before international flights
  • Check baggage allowances for different airlines
  • Consider travel insurance for international trips
  • Bring printed copies of booking confirmations
